What is the Fishbowl Game?

The best cheap party game

Fishbowl is a great "guess the word" style party game. It's ideal for medium sized groups.

Just want to read the rules? Check out our How to Play page to get started.

In Fishbowl, players take turns drawing cards from, you guessed it, a fishbowl. They have to get their team to guess which word they drew, but with certain restrictions. For example, the first round is usually played like taboo, where you can't say the word written on your card.

The game goes for three rounds, and each round gets more and more difficult!

Fishbowl is always a hit because it's cheap to play and really fun. All you need is a bowl and some paper! The different rounds provide a ton of variety, and there's even optional rounds you and all of your friends can play.

If you don't have your an empty goldfish bowl, don't fret. Any large bowl will suffice. We've heard some people call this game The Salad Bowl game, or the Goldfish Bowl game. Whatever you decide to call it, fishbowl is a super fun activity for any party.
