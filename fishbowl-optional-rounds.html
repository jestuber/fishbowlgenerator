Fishbowl Optional Rounds

The best cheap party game

If you love fishbowl, you have to try these hilarious optional rounds. Add them on to a normal game of fishbowl, or choose your own order!

Use our free Fishbowl Card Creator to create your cards.

Optional Round 1: Blanket

This round is just like the charades round, but with a twist: you have to act out everything while underneath a sheet! Try and find a large bedsheet, though a blanket will do in a pinch. Remember, you're still not allowed to talk, or make any noises (even if they are funny ghost sounds).

Optional Round 2: Pictionary

Time to test your drawing skills! Get out some paper and pens. Once the timer starts, draw out your word. Drawing words isn't allowed.

Optional Round 3: One Word

This round is like the 'One Sound' round, but you have to make your team guess by saying just one word! Once you've said your word, no more talking, noises, or motions. This round can be really tough, but you can do it with some cleverness.

Optional Round 4: Statue

Strike a pose! Think of how a statue would pose to represent your word. Once you've moved into position, freeze, just like a statue. You can't move until your team guesses the word!

Do you have any other fun ideas for Fishbowl rounds? Grab your friends, print off a new set of cards and play again!
